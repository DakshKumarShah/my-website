Famous Places

Palolem Beach

Pollem Beach is a sandy beach in Goa, India, that's known for its clear water, palm trees, and scenic beauty. It's located near the Karnataka border and is one of Goa's most beautiful beaches.

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is a famous beach in North Goa, India. It's known for its lively atmosphere, golden sands, and water sports.

Big Daddy Casino

Big Daddy Casino India is the largest floating casino in Asia, renowned for its grandeur and celebrity-filled events. With a wide range of popular casino games, including Indian Teen Patti and American Roulette, players of all levels can enjoy the thrill of gambling.

Famous Food

Goan fish curry

A staple of Goan cuisine, this dish is made with fish, coconut, and spices. It's also known as Xitti Kodi.

Prawn balchao

A pungent dish that originated during Goa's Portuguese colonization.

Pork vindaloo

A spicy curry that originated from a Portuguese sailor's dish. Goan cooks added palm vinegar, chili peppers, and spices.

Chicken cafreal

A spicy dish made with whole chicken legs, spices, and herbs. It originated from the Portuguese colonies in Africa.

Tradition

Apart from the Pre-Portuguese political history of Goa, Goa has a rich cultural heritage. Goa since ancient times celebrated a number of festivals such as the shigmo, the Zagor, Ganesh Chaturthi, Diwali etc. Many villages in Goa are noted for their peculiar rituals, folk festivals, folk dances, folk music etc.

About

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India within the Konkan region, geographically separated from the Deccan highlands by the Western Ghats. It is bound by the Indian states of Maharashtra to the north, and Karnataka to the east and south, with the Arabian Sea in the west. It is India's smallest state by area and fourth-smallest by population. Goa has the 2nd highest GDP per capita among all Indian states, two and a half times as high as the GDP per capita of the country as a whole. The Eleventh Finance Commission of India named Goa the best-placed state because of its infrastructure, and India's National Commission on Population rated it as having the best quality of life in India. It is the second-highest ranking among Indian states in the human development index.
